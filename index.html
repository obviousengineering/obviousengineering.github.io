Obvious Engineering is a research & development-driven
computer vision software company with a focus on consumer products.

Seene

Our 3D photography app  Seene  is available on iOS and Android and supports Google Cardboard for viewing in Virtual Reality. Seene’s 3D photo format adds depth, interaction and responsiveness to the existing social behaviour of mobile photo sharing. It was named as one of the best apps of the year by TechCrunch and has been downloaded more than 1.5 million times since its launch in October 2013.


Technology

We have developed a portfolio of technologies for use on low-powered mobile devices in real-time applications. We enable these devices to locate themselves in space, map visual environments, and recreate in 3D what is seen through the camera, turning a standard smartphone into a 3D scanner without the use of additional hardware or off-device processing.

Obvious Engineering’s technology was recognised by UKTI as the most innovative at Mobile World Congress 2014.
